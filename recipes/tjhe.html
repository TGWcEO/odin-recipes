The Juiciest Hamburger Ever

No more dry, lackluster hamburgers. These are juicy, and the spices can be easily
added or changed to suit anyone's taste. If you find the meat mixture too mushy,
just add more bread crumbs until it forms patties that hold thier shape.
  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Additional Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 35 mins
  • Serving: 8
  • Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds groud beef
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons evaporated milk
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/8 tablespoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat a grill for high heat.
  3. Mix the ground beef, and garlic in a large bowl using your hands.
  4. Form the mixture into 8 hamburger patties.
  5. Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill patties until browned and no longer pink, about 5 minutes per side.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
